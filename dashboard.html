Welcome to Your Home Inventory Manager!

This application helps you keep track of all your valuable possessions within your home. Organize items by room, track quantities, and estimate replacement costs, giving you peace of mind and a clear overview of your belongings. Whether for insurance purposes, personal organization, or simply knowing what you own, this tool is designed to simplify your home management.

**Important Insurance Advice:** Items valued over $3,000 (e.g., jewelry, furs, coins, collectibles, firearms) may need to be scheduled with your insurer. We highly recommend contacting your insurance provider to ask about scheduling these items and be ready to provide an **appraisal**.

Disclosure: This is a tool to assist you with staying organized for when life happens and you need to file a claim. This is not a guarantee to your payout, but a way to stay organized. Please note that a LICENSED adjuster and your insurer would have final say in claims.

How to Use This App

1. Manage Your Rooms and Items:

  • Navigate to the "Rooms" page (or similar link in your navigation).
  • There, you can add new items to specific rooms, or create new rooms if needed.
  • Each item can have a name, category, quantity, associated room, and an estimated replacement cost.
  • You can also edit or delete existing items from this section.

2. View Your Inventory:

  • The "Rooms" page provides a detailed breakdown of all your items, organized by the rooms they belong to.
  • You'll see a summary of total items and their collective estimated value.

3. Dashboard Overview (This Page):

  • This dashboard provides a quick welcome and guidance on how to use the main features of the application.
  • Your name (if logged in) is displayed in the header, and you can easily log out from here.

Click on the Inventory link to start adding your items!
