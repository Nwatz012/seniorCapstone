Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Login Issues

Q: I can't log in to my account A: First, try our Forgot Password feature. If you're still having trouble, make sure you're using the email address you registered with. Still need help? Contact us and we'll assist you.

Q: I'm not receiving password reset emails A: Check your spam/junk folder first. If you still don't see it, contact us with the email address you're trying to reset.

Managing Your Inventory

Q: How do I edit or delete an item? A: Go to your Inventory page, find the item you want to change, and click either the "Edit" or "Delete" button on the item card. For editing, update the information and click "Update Item" to save changes.

Q: Can I add items to multiple rooms at once? A: This feature is coming soon! For now, you'll need to add items to one room at a time through the Inventory page.

Q: What counts as 'proof of ownership'? A: Upload photos of the item itself, receipts, warranties, serial numbers, or any documentation that shows you own the item. This helps with insurance claims and personal records, but isn't required but highly suggested.

Photos & Receipts

Q: What file types can I upload? A: We support JPG, PNG, and PDF files. Perfect for item photos and digital receipts from online purchases.

Q: How many photos can I upload per item? A: You can upload multiple photos per item. We recommend taking photos from different angles and including any serial numbers or unique features.

Reports & Downloads

Q: My PDF report isn't downloading A: First, check if your browser is blocking popups - this can prevent the PDF from downloading. Try disabling ad blockers temporarily or using a different browser. If you're still having trouble, contact us as this may be a system issue.

Q: What's included in the PDF report? A: Your report includes all inventory items organized by room, with total values per room and an overall summary of your home information.

Insurance & Appraisals

Q: Do I need an appraisal for expensive items? A: Items valued over $3,000 (jewelry, furs, coins, collectibles, firearms) may need to be scheduled with your insurer as "scheduled personal property." Check your current home insurance policy to see the sublimits for specialty items. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to discuss coverage for high-value items.

Q: Will this app guarantee insurance payouts? A: This tool helps you stay organized and ready to file a claim, but it does not guarantee payout. Only a licensed adjuster and your insurer can determine coverage and reimbursement.

Data & Security

Q: Is my data secure? A: Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your inventory data is stored securely and is only accessible by you through your password-protected account.

Q: Can I backup or export my data? A: Data backup and export features are coming soon! For now, your PDF reports serve as a backup of your inventory information.

Q: How often should I update my inventory? A: We recommend updating your inventory whenever you make significant purchases, sell items, or at least once a year. This keeps your records current for insurance purposes.
